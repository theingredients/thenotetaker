1. Introduction

Welcome to The Note Taker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our standalone transcription application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Audio Data

Our application uses your device's microphone to capture audio for transcription purposes. This audio data is processed entirely within your browser using the Web Speech API and is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Important: When using browser-native speech recognition, your audio may be processed by your browser vendor's speech recognition services (Google, Microsoft, or Apple). Please review Section 5 for more information about third-party processing.

2.2 Transcript Data

Transcripts generated from your audio are stored temporarily in your browser's memory during your session. Transcripts are not automatically saved or transmitted to us. You may choose to download transcripts to your device, which are stored locally on your device only.

Warning: Transcripts may contain sensitive or confidential information. Please review transcripts carefully before downloading or copying them. We are not responsible for any disclosure of sensitive information contained in transcripts.

2.3 Audio Files

If you choose to upload audio files for transcription, these files are processed entirely within your browser. We do not upload, store, or transmit these files to any external servers. Audio files are limited to 100 megabytes (MB) in size and must be in a supported audio format.

2.4 Browser Information

Our application reads your browser's User Agent string to detect your browser type and version for compatibility checking. The User Agent string may contain information about your device, operating system, and browser version. This information is used locally within your browser and is not transmitted to our servers.

Note: Under certain privacy laws (such as GDPR), User Agent strings may be considered personal data. We use this information solely for compatibility purposes and do not store or transmit it.

2.5 Usage Data

We track estimated data usage locally in your browser to help you monitor your session. This data is not transmitted to us and is cleared when you close your browser session.

2.6 Clipboard Access

When you click the "Copy" button, our application writes transcript text to your device's clipboard using the Clipboard API. We do not read from your clipboard—we only write to it when you explicitly request to copy a transcript. Clipboard access requires browser permissions, which you may grant or deny.

2.7 Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use cookies or localStorage. However, we use sessionStorage solely to remember that you have accepted our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service during your browser session. This consent record is:

  • Stored only in your browser's sessionStorage (not persistent cookies)
  • Automatically cleared when you close your browser tab or window
  • Used only to remember your consent acceptance (does not contain personal data)
  • Not shared with any third parties

All other data exists only in your browser's memory during active sessions and is cleared when you close your browser or refresh the page.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since all processing occurs locally in your browser:

  • We do not have access to your audio recordings or transcripts
  • We do not store your personal information
  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes

4. Browser Permissions

Our application requires microphone access to provide transcription services. This permission is requested by your browser, and you may grant or deny it at any time through your browser settings. We only access your microphone when you explicitly start a recording session.

5. Third-Party Services and International Data Transfers

Our application uses browser-native APIs (Web Speech API and Web Audio API) provided by your browser vendor. These APIs may be subject to their own privacy policies:

  • Chrome/Edge: Google's Privacy Policy applies to Chrome's speech recognition service
  • Safari: Apple's Privacy Policy applies to Safari's speech recognition service

Please note that when using browser-native speech recognition, your audio may be processed by your browser vendor's speech recognition services. We recommend reviewing your browser vendor's privacy policy for details on how they handle audio data.

5.1 International Data Transfers

When you use our Service, your audio data may be processed by browser vendors (Google, Microsoft, or Apple) in countries outside your jurisdiction. These browser vendors may transfer and process your data in the United States or other countries where they operate.

We do not control how browser vendors handle your data. We recommend reviewing your browser vendor's privacy policy to understand:

  • Where your data is processed and stored
  • What data retention policies apply
  • What security measures are in place
  • Your rights regarding data processed by the browser vendor

By using our Service, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred internationally by browser vendors in accordance with their privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal data, you have full control over your information:

  • You can stop recording at any time
  • You can clear transcripts at any time
  • You can deny or revoke microphone permissions through your browser settings
  • You can delete downloaded transcripts from your device
  • You can download your transcripts (data portability)

8.1 GDPR Rights (European Union Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access (Article 15): You have the right to know what personal data we process. Since we do not collect or store your data, there is no data to access.
  • Right to Rectification (Article 16): You have the right to correct inaccurate data. Since we do not store your data, you can simply correct transcripts before downloading.
  • Right to Erasure (Article 17): You have the right to request deletion of your data. Since we do not store your data, simply clear your browser session or delete downloaded files.
  • Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18): You can stop using the Service at any time to prevent any processing.
  • Right to Data Portability (Article 20): You can download your transcripts at any time using the download feature.
  • Right to Object (Article 21): You can object to processing by not using the Service or by denying microphone permissions.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7): You can revoke microphone permissions at any time through your browser settings.

Data Controller: The Ingredients Collective (theingredientscollective@gmail.com) is the data controller for this Service. However, since we do not collect or store personal data, most GDPR rights are not applicable. For data processed by browser vendors, please contact them directly.

Legal Basis for Processing: Since we do not process personal data, no legal basis is required. For browser vendors processing your audio, their legal basis is governed by their privacy policies.

Automated Decision-Making: We do not use automated decision-making or profiling. Browser vendors may use automated processing for speech recognition, which is governed by their privacy policies.

8.2 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: You have the right to know what personal information we collect. As disclosed in this policy, we do not collect or store personal information.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. Since we do not store your data, simply clear your browser session or delete downloaded files.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information. If we were to sell personal information in the future, we would provide a "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise your California privacy rights, please contact us at theingredientscollective@gmail.com.

10. Security

While we do not transmit or store your data on our servers, we recommend:

  • Using our application over a secure (HTTPS) connection
  • Keeping your browser updated to the latest version
  • Reviewing your browser's privacy and security settings
  • Being cautious when downloading transcripts containing sensitive information
  • Reviewing transcripts before copying or downloading to ensure no sensitive data is exposed

Security Measures: Since all processing occurs in your browser, we rely on your browser's security features. We do not maintain servers that could be breached. However, you should be aware that:

  • Browser vendors process your audio data according to their security practices
  • Downloaded transcripts are stored on your device and subject to your device's security
  • Clipboard data may be accessible to other applications on your device

10.1 Data Breach Notification

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, the risk of a data breach affecting our systems is minimal. However, in the unlikely event that we become aware of a security incident affecting any data we may have access to, we will:

  • Investigate the incident promptly
  • Notify affected users within 72 hours (as required by GDPR) if personal data is affected
  • Notify relevant authorities as required by applicable law
  • Take steps to mitigate any harm

If you become aware of a security issue, please contact us immediately at theingredientscollective@gmail.com.
